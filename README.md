# Hometown Homepage - Delhi

A beautiful webpage showcasing Delhi, India, featuring top attractions, activities, and a local guide to help you explore the city.

## About

This project is a tribute to Delhi, highlighting its rich history, vibrant street food culture, and cultural landmarks. The page includes:

- **Hero Section**: An engaging introduction to visiting Delhi
- **Top Activities**: Three must-do experiences in Delhi:
  - Explore Ancient Heritage in Qutub Complex
  - Go for a Street Food & Heritage Walk in Old Delhi
  - Experience Cultural Immersion at Dilli Haat
- **Guide Section**: Meet your local guide, Mrinal Sood, who offers insider knowledge about the city

## Features

- Responsive design for all devices
- Clean, modern styling
- Image gallery showcasing Delhi's attractions
- Informative content about local attractions and activities

## Files

- `index.html` - Main webpage structure
- `styles.css` - Styling and layout
- `images/` - Image assets for the page

## How to Use

1. Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. Browse through the different sections to learn about Delhi's attractions
3. View images and descriptions of recommended activities
4. Learn more about your local guide
